My Hobbies

Beyond code and data, I find solace in poetry. Writing allows me to explore emotions, perspectives, and the human experience through carefully crafted words.

Original Poem

The Fool

Thou walkest a lonely path where the meager may wander,
Yet none dare lie beside thee.
"Fool," is the sound the world proclaims,
The name they cast upon thy voice.

So let the earth scorch beneath the heavens,
Let the skies rain blood upon the meek.
Then shall the meager know thy worth.
Yet still thou shalt remain—
The Fool.
